data privacy
how's your day takes the privacy and protection of your data seriously. This section outlines how we handle and safeguard any personal information you may provide while using our Random Quote Generator.

Data Collection and Usage

When you visit our website or use our PWA, we may collect certain information to provide you with the intended functionality and enhance your experience. This information may include: Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect non-personally identifiable information, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and usage statistics. This data is used to improve our services, analyze trends, and optimize performance.

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information without your explicit consent. If you choose to share personal information with us, such as your name or email address, it will only be used for the purpose you provided it. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These small text files are stored on your device and help us recognize you and remember your preferences. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that this may affect certain features or functionality of our website.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

Our PWA may integrate with third-party services, such as the Random.org API, to provide you with random quotes. These services have their own privacy policies and terms of service. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how your data is handled by these providers.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store personal information from children without verifiable parental consent. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child without proper consent, we will promptly delete it from our records.

try now!

how's your day aims to create a user-friendly Progressive Web Application (PWA) that caters to individuals seeking daily inspiration and motivation.

The current problem lies in the absence of a convenient platform that offers diverse and meaningful quotes. To address this, our app will leverage the random.org web service API to deliver a vast array of random quotes.

Users will have the ability to easily generate quotes with a single click, and share their favorite quotes with others. We define success for this project by achieving an active user base, receiving positive feedback, fostering high engagement rates, and witnessing an increase in the sharing of quotes on social media platforms.

Overall, our vision is to provide a seamless and inspiring experience for users, encouraging personal growth and well-being through the power of quotes.
